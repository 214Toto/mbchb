What is the difference in partial Pressure of CO2 between the tissues and in arterial blood ?
✓ The PCO2 in the tissues is higher than that in the arterial blood (about 46 and 40 mmHg at rest respectively).
What is the difference in partial Pressure of O2 between the tissues and in arterial blood ?
✓ the P02 in the arterial blood is higher than that in the tissues (about 95 and 40 mmHg at rest respectively)
What is the difference in partial Pressure of CO2 between the venous blood and in alveolar air ?
✓ The PCO2 in the venous blood is more than that in the alveolar air (about 46 and 40 mmHg at rest respectively).
What is the difference in partial Pressure of O2 between the alveolar air and the venous blood ?
✓ the P02 in the alveolar air is higher than that in the venous blood (about I00 and 40 mmHg at rest respectively).
